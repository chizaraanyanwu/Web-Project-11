The basics

To start learning chinese, you must understand the basics, the characters

Understanding the characters will teach you how to read chinese properly. most native speakers don't know all the characters but they can understand most by undertstanding what makes them up, their components.


Phrase Meaning Pronunciation
你好 Hello, hi Ni Hao (knee how)
早上好 Good morning, literal morning good Zao Shang Hao (Zaw Shang Haow)
再见 Goodbye, literal again see (Hope to see you again) Zai Jian (Zai Jian)
